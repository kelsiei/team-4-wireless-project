# Paper Summary - Comprehensive analysis on 5G and 6G wireless network security and privacy 
  Full Citation: Rajesh, K., and P. Vetrivelan. "Comprehensive analysis on 5G and 6G wireless network security and privacy." Telecommunication Systems 88 (2025): 52.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
  ## Summary
This research provides an overview of the security and privacy challenges of 6G. The authors propose a Quantum-Enhanced Blockchain Framework (QEBF) to defend Intrustion Detection Systems against 
adversarial machine learning attacks, such as poisoning and evasion. Through simulations, the paper demonstrates how adversarial examples can reduce model performance and how integrating blockchain 
and quantum principles can restore model integrity.                                                                                                              

  ## Relevance to our Survey
This source is very relevant as it directly addressess our team's concern that attackers can poison data to trick 6G AI systems to distrupt the network. It also validates the project's focus on "Zero Trust" defenses that 
utilize AI for continuous verification.                                                                                                                       

  ## An Issue I Identified
While the QEBF reduces the impact of adversarial attacks, the authors note training times increased for every model when the framework was applied. They also acknowledge that Quantum Machine Learning models have not yet 
been tested within it.
