Security Threats and AI Defenses in 6G
https://github.com/kelsiei/team-4-wireless-project

Justification: In 6G networks, AI will automatically handle critical tasks like directing data and managing network resources. While this makes 6G incredibly fast, it also creates a major weakness: attackers can "poison" the data to trick the AI and disrupt the entire system. Because 6G is decentralized, old security methods no longer work. This project investigates the battle between these AI-based attacks and new "Zero Trust" defenses that use AI to constantly verify that every part of the network is safe. Our goal is to find the best ways to keep 6G running securely, even when its own AI is being targeted.
