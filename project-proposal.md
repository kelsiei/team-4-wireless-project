## 3. Candidate References

* **Security risks and countermeasures of adversarial attacks on AI-driven applications in 6G networks: A survey**
    * [Link](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/384161068_Security_risks_and_countermeasures_of_adversarial_attacks_on_AI-driven_applications_in_6G_networks_A_survey)
    * ML threats specific to 6G intelligence and corresponding defense frameworks.

* **Defense strategies for Adversarial Machine Learning: A survey**
    * [Link](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/373083122_Defense_strategies_for_Adversarial_Machine_Learning_A_survey)
    * Reactive and proactive defense mechanisms against a wide range of adversarial attacks.

* **De-Pois: An attack-agnostic defense against data poisoning attacks**
    * [Link](https://www.chenwang.net.cn/publications/De-Pois-TIFS21.pdf)
    * Detecting and filtering poisoned training data without knowledge of the attack method.

* **Cyber-Security in the 6G Era: Emerging Threats, Multi-Layered Defense, and Future Directions**
    * [Link](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.14780)
    * 6G threat surfaces and the necessity of multilayered defensive architectures.

* **A Survey on Zero Trust Architecture for 6G**
    * [Link](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/stamp/stamp.jsp?arnumber=10288499)
    * Zero Trust principles and AI driven continuous verification in 6G environments.
